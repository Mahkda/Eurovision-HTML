Eurovision Statistics

Youtube views

While, obviously not accurate, the number of views on an Eurovision official song video is a good proxy on how popular and/or trending one of the participant song is. This value does have limits, as populous countries are more likely to have a video with more views as people from a country are the most likely to watch a specific country song. And also different countries have different relation to eurovision, countries more invested in eurovision are more likely to watch their own country eurovision song, and it does benefit song release long ago rather than recent ones.

Rate of change of views

The rate of change of views is another proxy of how trendy a song is. As it is a percentage of change this metric favors recently released official videos.
